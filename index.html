Volcano Safety Tips

Exploring volcanic regions requires special precautions. Here are some essential safety tips for volcano tourism:

  • Always follow your guide's instructions
  • Wear appropriate footwear and protective gear
  • Stay on marked paths and trails
  • Be aware of gas emissions and changing conditions
  • Carry necessary supplies including water and first aid
